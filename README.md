### 📱 About the Project

**Tracklife** is a comprehensive native Android application designed to manage personal health and wellness. 

Developed as the **Final Capstone Project** for the **Mobile Technologies Course**, this app demonstrates the power of modern Android development using **Jetpack Compose** for a fully declarative UI. It goes beyond simple tracking by integrating **Artificial Intelligence** to streamline the management of medical appointments.

> *The goal: To consolidate physical activity tracking, vital signs monitoring, and medical scheduling into a single, intuitive interface.*

---

### 🤖 The AI Edge

What sets Tracklife apart is its intelligent approach to scheduling:
- **Smart Appointments**: Leverages AI to assist users in scheduling, organizing, and managing medical consultations efficiently, reducing the friction of manual booking.

---

### 🌟 Key Features

The app is structured around specific user needs, utilizing a clean navigation flow:

- **🏠 Home Dashboard**: 
  - Personalized greetings based on time/date.
  - Quick-view cards for daily steps, next appointments, and heart rate trends.
- **❤️ Health Monitor**: 
  - **Vital Signs**: Log and visualize Heart Rate and Blood Pressure.
  - **Medication**: (Placeholder) System to manage prescriptions and intakes.
  - **Reports**: (Placeholder) Digital archive for medical results.
- **🏃 Activity Tracking**: 
  - Visual progress rings for daily steps, calories burned, and distance.
  - Task management system for fitness goals ("Pending" vs "Completed").
- **📅 Medical Appointments**: 
  - Distinction between Virtual and In-person visits.
  - Detailed view with doctor info, specialty, and location.
- **👤 Profile & Settings**: 
  - User configuration and customization options.
